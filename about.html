About Stractural Deep Tissue Massage

The Natural Way To Feel Better

Structural Massage, also known as Structural Integration, is a form of bodywork that focuses on the fascia, the connective tissue that surrounds muscles, bones, and organs in the body. This technique involves a full body range of motion (ROM) assessment followed by work to increase joint motion using static pressure, static pressure with passive and active motion, and active resistance. The goal of Structural Massage is to release and reorganize the body’s fascia, helping to reduce chronic pain, soreness, and tension.
